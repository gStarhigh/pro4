Who are we?


Our Instructor

Meet Maria, a distinguished dressage rider whose journey has transcended the Grand Prix arena to empower a new generation of enthusiasts. Having conquered the intricacies of Grand Prix competitions, Maria now channels her expertise into teaching. With a discerning eye for detail and a nurturing approach, she guides her students towards mastery, emphasizing the symbiotic partnership between rider and horse.

Maria's legacy extends beyond medals; she molds well-rounded equestrians. Her lessons are a fusion of classical wisdom and modern insights, fostering not only technical skill but also a profound appreciation for the art of dressage. As a beacon of dedication, Maria inspires her students to embrace values of care and empathy, ensuring her impact resonates far beyond the arena, shaping the very essence of the equestrian world.

The ridetrack

Nestled under the night sky, our lit-up riding arena is a place where magic happens. It's a spacious 20x60 meters, glowing softly with lights that make it come alive after dark. This is where riders and their horses team up to create something beautiful. The special lighting makes everything look like a graceful dance, whether it's trotting, cantering, or even jumping over obstacles.

But it's not just a pretty sight, it's a fun place to play and learn. There are different things to jump over, like hurdles and bars, which makes practicing super exciting. No matter if you're new to riding or really experienced, our arena is like a big playground where you and your horse can become the best of buddies. As it's so spacious, it's possible to ride more than 1 at a time. With our Instructors instruction, this ridetrack will become your next home!

The Summer Pasture

During the sunny months from May to October, our enchanting summer pasture becomes a haven for young horses aged 1 to 3 years old. With nature as their playground, up to 10 playful foals and young horses roam freely on about 6 hectares of lush, green land. It's like a horse wonderland where they can stretch their legs, make new friends, and just be themselves.

Our team keeps a close watch on these young stars, visiting them at least twice a day to make sure they're happy and healthy. Rain or shine, we're there to ensure they have everything they need. The changing weather decides how long they get to enjoy this horsey paradise, but whether it's a long summer or a shorter one, these young horses make the most of every moment. It's like a summer camp for them, full of fun, growth, and the kind of memories that last a lifetime.

The Stable

Nestled next to this vibrant pasture is our cozy barn, a home away from home for your companions. With six spacious stalls, it's a haven of comfort and security. Each horse has their own space, a place where they can rest and recharge after a day of adventure in the pasture. We understand that horses are unique individuals, so we make sure they have everything they need, from cozy bedding to fresh water, ensuring they're content and at ease.

We have two dedicated grooming areas complete with grooming supplies and a convenient water trough. Our barn also features two spacious tack rooms, perfect for storing all your riding gear and equipment. With plenty of room to organize saddles, bridles, and other essentials, you can rest assured that your tack is well-cared-for and ready whenever you are. When it comes to the daily essentials, our team has you covered. Feeding, mucking out stalls, and turning horses in and out are all part of the routine. It's all about creating a seamless and stress-free experience for both you and your horse, so you can focus on enjoying your time together.

In this carefully designed space, both horses and riders find a place where their needs are met and their dreams can flourish. From the spacious stalls to the well-equipped tack rooms, every detail is designed with care to ensure that your time at our barn is filled with joy and memorable moments.

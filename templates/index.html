What We Offer


Dressage Lessons

Get personalized instruction from one of our experienced instructors, regardless of your skill level.

Our dressage lessons are designed to help you improve your riding skills and build a strong relationship with your horse. We offer lessons for all levels, from beginners to advanced riders.

Fully Equipped Stable

6 Spacious Stable Boxes with All the Amenities

Our stables are spacious and well-ventilated, with plenty of room for your horse to move around. They are also equipped with all the amenities your horse needs, including fresh water and plenty of hay.

Incredible Service

We are committed to providing your horse with the best possible care.

We offer both in-turn and out-turn options for your horse. In-turn means that your horse will be turned out in our paddocks during the day and brought back to the stable at night. Out-turn means that your horse will be out in our paddocks 24/7.

Big Meadows

Lush Pastures and Open Spaces for Your Horse to Enjoy

Our meadows are located in a beautiful setting and they offer your horse plenty of room to run and play. The grass is lush and green, and the air is fresh and clean. Your horse will love spending time in our meadows.

Increadible Summer Pasture

Our summer pasture is the perfect place for your horse to relax and rejuvenate.

Our summer pasture is located in a peaceful setting and it offers your horse plenty of room to graze and relax. The grass is lush and green, and the air is fresh and clean. The pasture is around 4acres and your horse will love it!

Value-for-money boarding

Our Boarding is Cost-Effective and Offers a High Level of Care

We have a variety of boarding options to fit your budget and your horse's needs. We also offer a variety of other services, such as turnout, feeding and cleaning the boxes.
